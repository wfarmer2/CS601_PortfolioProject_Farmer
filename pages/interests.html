Interests

  • Sports

    I have been an avid sports fan ever since my childhood. Being raised in Arizona has given me the opportunity to watch many Phoenix Suns games in person and hopefully they will win the championship in my lifetime. I played basketball from 5th grade until the end of high school and plan to coach one day. I also enjoy watching football and participate in a fantasy football league. When there are sports on television you can usually find me watching it!

  • Formula 1 Racing

    During the pandemic I found myself looking for new content to watch and stumbled across Formula 1 racing and have been a fan ever since. My favorite team is Red Bull Racing with my favorite driver being that of Max Verstappen! I plan on attending a Grand Prix one day, either in the United States of America or Canada.

  • Movies

    I love everything about movies and movie culture especially learning about behind the scenes facts how a movie is made. One of my fondest memories has been experiencing movies with my family growing up and going to midnight premieres for the newest film that everyone is interested in such as Marvel, Star Wars, etc. Fun fact, I saw Star Wars Episode III five times in theaters as a kid when that movie was released!

  • Baking

    Another interest that I picked up during the pandemic was baking that was fueled by watching all the baking shows that were on television. I have baked several batches of cupcakes and brownies over the last couple of years. A tradition that has started because of my interest in baking is that every holiday season I make Christmas light cupcakes.
